    • Nepathya Concert

    📅 July 25, 2025 | 🕒 5:00 PM - 8:00 PM | 📍 Baneshwor, KTM

    Get ready for an extraordinary musical journey as Nepathya, Nepal's most revered folk-rock band, takes the stage for an unforgettable live concert! This highly anticipated event promises an immersive experience where their iconic melodies, deeply rooted in Nepali folk traditions yet infused with contemporary rock energy, will resonate through the venue. Attendees can look forward to a powerful and soulful performance, celebrating Nepathya's rich musical legacy and their unique ability to connect with audiences through their poignant lyrics and captivating stage presence. It's an absolute must-attend for anyone who appreciates authentic Nepali music and a truly memorable concert atmosphere.
