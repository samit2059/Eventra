    • About Eventra

    Eventra is more than a project it's our way of bringing people together through meaningful experiences. Built with a vision to modernize how Nepal discovers and hosts events, Eventra blends simplicity, security, and creativity.

    Our Mission

    To simplify event discovery and management for everyone from small workshops to major festivals by offering an all-in-one online platform.

    Our Vision

    To be Nepal's go-to event ecosystem, empowering both attendees and organizers with tools that make participation effortless and exciting.

    What We Offer

    Secure ticket booking via eSewa, custom host dashboards, beautiful event displays, and a seamless platform for event engagement.

    Who We Are

    A passionate group of four students: Crystal, Samit, Bijay & Susan, who believe in learning by building, and growing by collaborating.
